Donation Rules and Suggestions

    Recommended:

  • Fresh produce
  • Dairy products (cheese milk eggs)
  • Raw Meat * ONLY IN BOTTOM RACK OF FREEZER *
  • Pantry items
  • Canned goods
  • Cleaning supplies

    • Guidelines for homemade food include:

  • Made date
  • Ingredients
  • Estimated expiration
  • Use a tightly sealed container
  • Make sure there is a way to tell if the container has been opened already (such as taping down all four sides or the top of the bag)

    • Not Allowed:

  • Alcohol
  • Unlabeled or opened meals
  • Prepared and packaged foods that do not follow food donation guidelines
  • Partially consumed packages
